How it works

1

Click an event

Open any event in Google Calendar.

2

It asks Google

Since When?! fetches the details in the background.

3

See the answer

Whomst and Whenst - right in the popup.

Privacy: Since When?! reads your Google Calendar data to display creator info. Nothing is stored, sent to third parties, or logged by us. The extension only talks to Google's servers, using your existing Google account.
